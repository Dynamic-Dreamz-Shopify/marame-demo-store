Our Story

Marame was vision born out of years of frustration, not with fashion itself, but with what it’s become: overcomplicated, trend-driven, price-obsessed, and too often missing the one thing that matters — longevity.
For as long as I can remember, I’ve been stuck in the same cycle: buy something new, wear it non-stop for two weeks, and then watch it drift to the back of the wardrobe… until it eventually ended up in the annual charity shop bag. The issue wasn’t that the clothes were bad — they just didn’t work. They didn’t go with anything else I owned, I didn’t know how to style them differently, and after the initial buzz wore off, they felt like more clutter than clothing.
What I needed didn’t really exist: a wardrobe built around timeless, versatile pieces that were designed to work together. Not just in terms of style, but in fit, colour, quality, and wearability. Things that felt great on, looked effortless, and could be worn over and over again without feeling tired. Beautiful, flattering shapes. Complementary colours. Soft, textured, high-quality fabrics. Smart pairings. Classic cuts. Simple, right? Apparently not.
But, I have a tendency to gravitate towards complex situations, so I started building it.

2.5 Years, 452 Samples, and One Very Specific Button

Creating Marame has been a two and a half year journey of obsessive detail and determination. I didn’t want to throw together a few basics in a cardboard box and call it timeless and sustainable. I wanted every part of the collection to feel considered. That meant researching fabrics from mills around the world, comparing swatches under different light, testing weights, drapes, and textures until we found the right ones.
We said no — a lot. To suppliers who didn’t meet our standards. To manufacturers who couldn’t guarantee ethical and sustainable practices. To materials that looked good but didn’t wear well. To packaging that didn’t smell very nice. After what felt like a global search, we finally found production partners who genuinely care about quality and craft — and it shows.
From there, we worked, reworked, and worked some more. Patterns were drafted, redrafted, and tested across different body types to ensure every piece fit how it should. We laboured over small details — the curve of a neckline, the depth of a hem, the exact weight of a button (yes, that happened). At one point, we had over 40 iterations of a single design.
It was long. It was intense. And occasionally, borderline ridiculous. But it was worth it!

The Marame Approach

Marame is built around the idea of a purposeful wardrobe — pieces that make getting dressed easier, not harder. Clothes that don’t rely on trends to feel relevant. Staples you can mix, match, and re-style without second-guessing. Garments that feel as good as they look — and that stay in your wardrobe, not in a landfill.
We’re not interested in overwhelming drops or chasing the next thing. Every piece we create is designed with intention, made to last, and produced responsibly. We put thought into everything — from the fabric, fit and finish, to the experience of wearing it, living in it, and loving it for years to come.

The Start of Something Long-Lasting

If you’ve ever felt uninspired by your wardrobe, or frustrated by clothes that looked great for a week and never again, you’re not alone. Marame was made for people like you — people who value quality, simplicity, and style that stands the test of time.
This is just the beginning. And we’re so glad to have you with us.
